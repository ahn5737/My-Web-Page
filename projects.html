Light Control Gate Using PIR Sensors

How nice would it be if the lights turn on automatically as I enter my room and turn off when I exit? To make this happen, I picked up a Microcontroller (Texas Instruments MSP430 LaunchPad), few light bulbs, and two pyroelectric sensors to make a sensor gated light control system.
